Frequently Asked Questions



Is riding with Ski Lift safe?

Absolutely. Ski Lift screens all drivers, accepting only those who are at least
18 years of age and have a valid license and vehicle insurance.

Selecting a vehicle option for your ride

You can see all vehicle options available in your area before confirming your ride.
Several vehicle options are offered to get you to the slopes safely and comfortably.

NOTE: Once a ride request is made, the vehicle choice cannot be changed without cancelling
and requesting the ride again.

Requesting more than one vehicle

Using your Ski Lift account, one vehicle may be requested at a time. If you are travelling
with a group too large for one vehicle, please ask others in your group to use their Ski Lift
accounts to request additional vehicles.

Riding With Pets

Please let your Ski Lift driver know if you will be travelling with any pets.

Lost/Forgotten Items

Ski Lift allows you to communicate with your driver even after your trip has been completed.
If an item needs to be retrieved, please contact your driver.
